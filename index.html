Behavioral Model

Four Precision States

WeffAI categorizes desk activity into actionable, realistic states — no binary "active/idle" oversimplification.

Working

Worker present and actively engaged — physical movement confirms hands-on task execution.

Passive Working

Present but still for 2+ min. Logged as productive — reading, training videos, deep thinking qualify.

Distracted

Phone usage or off-task behavior detected. Flagged without invasive wrist-level tracking.

Away

Worker left workstation boundary for 120+ seconds — body center and hands absent from zone.
